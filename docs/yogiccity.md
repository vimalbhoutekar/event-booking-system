## 1. Platform Overview & Business Model

### 1.1 Problem Statement

The traditional yoga education market faces several challenges:

- Students struggle to find qualified instructors in their locality
- Instructors lack a centralized platform to reach potential students
- No standardized booking and payment system
- Difficulty in verifying instructor credentials
- Limited transparency in pricing and reviews

### 1.2 Solution

A comprehensive marketplace platform that:

- Enables location-based instructor discovery
- Provides standardized booking and payment systems
- Implements verified instructor profiles with ratings/reviews
- Offers flexible class formats (in-studio, home visits, online)
- Facilitates transparent communication between instructors and students

### 1.3 Business Model Selection

**Selected Model: Commission-Based Marketplace** âœ…

#### How It Works:

1. Instructors list their classes on the platform
2. Students discover and book classes
3. Payment is processed through the platform
4. Platform deducts commission (15%)
5. Instructor receives remaining amount (85%)

#### Example Transaction Flow:

```
Class Price: â‚¹500
Platform Commission (15%): â‚¹75
Instructor Receives: â‚¹425
Payment Schedule: Weekly settlements every Friday
```

#### Why This Model?

**Advantages:**

- Low operational complexity - no inventory management
- Scalable architecture - easy to add new instructors
- Instructor autonomy - freedom to set prices and schedules
- Lower financial risk - no upfront payments to instructors
- Industry standard - proven model (Urban Company, Sulekha)

**Comparison with Alternatives:**

| Feature                 | Commission Model | Managed Platform     | Listing Only |
| ----------------------- | ---------------- | -------------------- | ------------ |
| Platform Control        | Medium           | High                 | Low          |
| Revenue Stream          | Per booking      | Subscription/Booking | Advertising  |
| Instructor Independence | High             | Low                  | Very High    |
| Quality Control         | Medium           | High                 | Low          |
| Complexity              | Low              | High                 | Very Low     |
| Scalability             | High             | Medium               | High         |

**Selected Approach: Real Payment Gateway in MVP** âœ…

- Indian market optimized with UPI support
- Easy integration with comprehensive documentation
- Supports multiple payment methods (UPI, Cards, NetBanking, Wallets)
- Automatic settlement features
- PCI DSS compliant
